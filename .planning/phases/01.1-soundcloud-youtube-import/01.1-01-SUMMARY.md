## Decisions Made
- ConcurrentDictionary for volatile download progress data (not persisted in Track model) -- avoids polluting the model with transient state
- 2 concurrent downloads max (SemaphoreSlim) based on bandwidth constraint from research
- Title parsing: regex strips common video suffixes, then splits on dash/en-dash/em-dash for Artist - Title separation
- Duplicate URL imports allowed with warning (per user decision in plan context)
